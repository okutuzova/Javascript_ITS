playGame() → {void}

Starts and manages the Hangman game loop. The function continuously makes guesses until the player either: - Wins by guessing all letters correctly. - Loses by reaching the maximum allowed wrong guesses. It ensures that a win/loss message is always displayed at the end of the game.
