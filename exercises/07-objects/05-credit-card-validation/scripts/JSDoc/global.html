validateCreditCard(cardNumberString) → {Object}

Validates the credit card number based on specific rules. 1. Card number must have exactly 16 digits. 2. The card must contain at least two different digits. 3. The final digit must be even. 4. The sum of the digits must be greater than 16.
Parameters:
